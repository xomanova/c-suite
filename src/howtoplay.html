HOW TO PLAY

Discover the saboteurs before your company folds!

Players: 5-10
Each player is dealt a random role at the start of the game.
Roles have unique actions. Any role can be an employee or saboteur.

Keep your company objectives hidden!
Employees know nothing.
Saboteurs know the other saboteurs.

At the start of the game, a timer starts with 10 minutes of company status.
Some actions can add or remove from the counter.
When the time reaches 5 and 0 minutes a vote is triggered for all players.
The saboteurs must be correctly identified by the majority of employees.
If the vote fails, saboteurs remain unknown and the company folds - saboteurs win.

Each game begins with a random action, the player with the corresponding role plays first.

Roles:

All roles are unique in a 5 player game.
For games with 6 or more players, an equal amount of "Middle Management" cards are added to the deck for random selection.

CEO - Chief Executive Officer

1 per game
Action: Make a Statement
Action Description: Choose the next determinate action and successfully predict the outcome (Pass/Fail)
Selectable Actions: Bookkeeping, Plan, Mediate
The outcome prediction remains hidden, but the CEO may choose to share.
Pass: The outcome was successfully predicted - You have bought the company time! +15 seconds
Fail: The outcome predication was unsuccessful - Confidence in your leadership erodes. - 15 seconds

CFO - Chief Financial Officer

1 per game
Action: Bookkeeping
Action Description: Successfully identify two members with the same faction. Identies remain hidden.
You cannot select yourself.
Pass: You successfully identified two players. - You have bought the company time! +15 seconds
Fail: Your selection was inaccurate - The numbers don't add up! -15 seconds

COO - Chief Operating Officer

1 per game
Action: Plan
Action Description: Shuffle leadership. Propose a vote to block a player for 1 minute.
You cannot select yourself.
Pass: The vote succeeds. - The players action is blocked for 1 minute.
Fail: Poor planning... - 30 seconds

HR - Chief Human Resources Officer

1 per game
Action: Mediate
Action Description: Successfully identify one employee and one saboteur on the board. Identies remain hidden.
You cannot select yourself.
Pass: You successfully identified two players. - You have bought the company time! +30 seconds
Fail: Your selection was inaccurate. - Mediation failed! -30 seconds

CSO - Chief Security Officer

1 per game
Action: Create Policy
Action Description: You must select an action to wrap in endless policies for the next minute. The selected role cannot be played for the duration.
You cannot select yourself.
Only determinate actions can be selected: Make a Statement, Bookkeeping, Plan, Mediate

Middle Management - Middle Managers

0-5 per game
Action: Meetings
Action Description: Middle managers vote to swap factions of two members of the board. Majority wins!
Votes can fail.
Swapped factions are not revealed.
This action can only occur once per game.
