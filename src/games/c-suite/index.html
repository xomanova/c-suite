C-Suite

A game about the company you keep

  • 5-10
  • deceit
  • deduction
  • teams

The clash between the Saboteurs and the Employees is putting the company under threat. Each side wishes to move the line in keeping with their own agenda; if the Saboteurs can confuse the employees for long enough, the company will fold. Employees must work together to uncover the crafty Saboteurs. Although the Saboteurs are outnumbered, they also remain hidden and collaborate in the shadows by subverting the actions and decisions of unsuspecting employees. Trust no one!
