GAME
C-Suite is a new social deduction game designed to be played with 4-10 players. It makes use of common game mechanics such as factions, role cards, voting phases and deception.

The site is entirely hosted and deployed through managed services, if that's something that interests you please please feel free to browse the repository for this project!
